About Us

Our journey of excellence in accounting education spans over a decade with global reach.

Our Journey

We have been empowering students in Accounting and Reporting since 2012, building a strong foundation of knowledge and expertise in the field.

Our History

  • Founded in 2012 in Dubai, bringing premium accounting education to students
  • Expanded our presence to Chennai and Bangalore in India
  • Since 2017, enriched our curriculum with ACCA(UK) and Dip IFR(UK)
  • Currently headquartered in Whitefield, Bangalore for in-person classes
  • Offering online tutoring across India and the Middle East

Our Expertise

  • Led by a Founder with extensive teaching background and industry knowledge
  • Professional trainers with experience at PricewaterhouseCoopers (PwC)
  • Faculty with World Bank Group experience, bringing global insights
  • Specialized in early accounting education for students from grade 11
  • Corporate training providers for professionals seeking Dip IFR certification

Our Approach

Educational Philosophy

We believe in igniting a passion for Accounting and Reporting from an early stage. Our mentoring approach begins with students in grade 11, building a strong foundation that carries them through their educational journey and professional career.

Professional Development

Beyond student education, we're committed to professional growth. Our corporate training programs for Diploma in International Financial Reporting (Dip IFR) help working professionals enhance their skills and advance their careers in the financial sector.
